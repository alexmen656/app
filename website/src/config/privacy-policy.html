This Privacy Policy explains how KalBuddy ("KalBuddy", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses and protects your information when you use the KalBuddy mobile application and related services (the "App"). We care about your privacy and follow a simple principle: we only collect what we need to make the App work, and we keep as much of your data as possible on your own device.

By using the App you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information we collect

Information you provide. When you set up your profile we ask for details such as your name, age, gender, height, current and target weight, activity level and nutrition goals. You may also send us feedback or support messages.

Health and nutrition data. To deliver its core features the App processes the food you log, photos of your meals and product barcodes, the nutritional values (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat), your weight entries and your streaks and progress over time.

Meal photos. When you take a photo of a meal or a nutrition label, the image is sent to our analysis service so that our AI can identify the food and estimate its nutritional content. Images are used only to generate your result and are not used to advertise to you or sold to third parties.

Purchase information. Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat). We receive your subscription status and related transaction information, but we never receive or store your full payment-card details.

Automatically collected information. Like most apps, we may collect limited technical and diagnostic data such as device type, operating system version, app version and crash or performance information to keep the App stable and secure.

How we use your information

We use your information to provide and operate the App's features (food recognition, tracking, analytics and insights), to personalise your calorie and macro goals, to process your subscription, to respond to your feedback and support requests, to improve and secure the App, and to comply with our legal obligations.

Where your data is stored

KalBuddy is built to be local-first: your profile, food logs, weight entries and history are stored primarily on your own device. Meal photos are transmitted to our analysis service to produce your nutrition result and are not retained longer than necessary for that purpose. Subscription data is processed by Apple and RevenueCat.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you choose to enable Apple Health syncing, the App can read and write nutrition-related data (such as calories and macronutrients) to and from Apple Health. This is entirely optional and only happens with your explicit permission, which you can revoke at any time in your device settings. Data obtained through HealthKit is used solely to provide health and fitness features within the App. It is never used for advertising, and it is never shared with or sold to third parties.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the App, including Apple (App Store, in-app purchases and, optionally, HealthKit), RevenueCat (subscription management), Open Food Facts (product and barcode data) and our hosting and AI analysis infrastructure. These providers process data only as needed to deliver their service to us, under their own privacy terms.

Legal bases for processing

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we process your data on the following legal bases: performance of our contract with you (to provide the App and your subscription), your consent (for example for Apple Health syncing and meal-photo analysis), our legitimate interests (to secure and improve the App) and compliance with legal obligations.

Data retention

We keep your data only for as long as needed to provide the App and for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law. Data stored on your device is removed when you delete your data or uninstall the App. You can request deletion of any data we hold at any time.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export your personal data, to restrict or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent you have previously given. If you are in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Deleting your data

You can delete your locally stored data at any time from within the App or by uninstalling it. To request deletion of any account or server-side data associated with you, contact us at support@kalbuddy.com and we will action your request without undue delay.

Children's privacy

KalBuddy is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

International data transfers

Some of our providers may process data outside your country of residence. Where data is transferred internationally, we take steps to ensure it remains protected by appropriate safeguards in line with applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" shown above and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@kalbuddy.com.
